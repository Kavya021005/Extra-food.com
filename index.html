What We Do

Extra Food Collection connects wedding halls, caterers, and event organizers with NGOs, shelters, and volunteers to redistribute leftover food efficiently.

Wedding Halls

Venues register surplus food available after events.

Caterers

Caterers list leftover prepared food for quick pickup.

NGOs & Shelters

Organizations receive food notifications and claim pickups.

Volunteers

Volunteers coordinate pickup and delivery of food.

Event Organizers

Organizers post surplus food from any large event.
