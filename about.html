What We Do

Extra Food Collection connects event organizers with NGOs, shelters, and volunteers to redistribute surplus food — reducing waste and feeding those in need.

Caterers

Volunteers

Volunteers coordinate pickup and delivery of food.

Event Organizers

Organizers post surplus food from any large event.
